Project: Feedback - MD

Frontend:
- Clean responsive staff feedback form.
- Shows Name, Office, Email as read-only fields from Blink SSO.
- Staff enters Feedback only.
- Shows success/error/warning messages.
- Works on desktop and mobile.

Backend:
- Node.js Express app.
- SAML SSO with Blink.
- API route: /api/me
- API route: /api/feedback
- Sends email to admin@multidynamic.com.au
- Saves backup submissions to data/feedback-submissions.jsonl

Recommended domain:
https://feedback.multidynamic.com.au
